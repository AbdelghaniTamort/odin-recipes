Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish composed of layers of rich, flavorful ingredients. It begins with a hearty meat sauce made from ground beef, onions, garlic, tomatoes, and Italian herbs, simmered until thick and aromatic. This sauce is alternated with sheets of tender lasagna pasta and a creamy, velvety mixture of ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan. Each layer is generously topped with shredded mozzarella cheese, which melts and bubbles to a golden perfection in the oven. The lasagna is baked until the top is beautifully browned and crispy, and the layers meld together in a harmonious blend of textures and flavors, resulting in a comforting, savory masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
